TAKE ME HOME

cherry for four years

HOW LONG HAVE YOU BEEN DOING DRAG FOR ?
I have been doing drag since I was 19. It started with doing amateur pantomime. I have only been Cherry for the last 4 years it took a long time to build a character and find something that worked.

HAVE YOU SEEN A CHANGE IN THE AUDIENCES YOU PERFORM TO SINCE YOU STARTED ?
I don’t think there has been a huge change in the audience since I’ve performed. I perform to a range of audiences but don’t feel they have overly changed.

AT WHAT POINT DO YOU BECOME YOUR DRAG CHARACTER ? IS IT BEFORE YOU GO ON OR WHEN YOU’RE ON THE STAGE ?
I become my drag character when I have finished getting ready. Once I am in full drag I become different and adopt her persona. I see myself as constantly performing when in drag regardless if I’m on stage or stood at the bar

DO YOU ONLY DO DRAG TO PERFORM OR DO YOU GO OUT IN FULL DRAG ELSEWHERE?
I only do drag as a career and don’t attend parties or dress in drag as a hobby.

DO YOU FEEL DRAG CULTURE HAS RISES AND FALLS ? FOR INSTANCE A LOT OF YOUNGER PEOPLE ARE STARTING TO APPRECIATE DRAG CULTURE MORE AND BE MORE OPEN ABOUT THE BLURRING OF GENDER BOUNDARIES.
I do think drag culture has rises and falls. U.K. Drag culture is very different to US Drag culture but has been extremely influenced by shows like RuPaul’s Drag Race. Although it’s great that drag is somewhat more mainstream now it also faces its challenges as people now feel as though they have an opinion. For drag queens the scene is very competitive and you are constantly pressured to look good, perform well, keep up your persona and be a strong gay activist at the same time.
