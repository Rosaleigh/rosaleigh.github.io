TAKE ME HOME

gender boundaries

HOW LONG HAVE YOU BEEN DOING DRAG ?
7 years
HAVE YOU SEEN A CHANGE IN THE AUDIENCES YOU PERFORM TO SINCE YOU STARTED?
In most cases yes but not everywhere, there has been an influx of younger audiences watching drag now.
AT WHAT POINT DO YOU BECOME YOUR DRAG CHARACTER ? IS IT BEFORE YOU GO ON OR WHEN YOU’RE ON THE STAGE?
The minute my make up and wig is on I feel in character and then when I’m about to go on stage the character's already there.

♥ ♥ ♥ ♥ ♥ ♥

DO YOU ONLY DO DRAG TO PERFORM OR DO YOU GO OUT ELSEWHERE IN FULL DRAG?
Drag is just a performance-based thing for me now but when I first started I used to walk around soho in drag and go to the bars for fun. Now it’s a job.
DO YOU FEEL DRAG CULTURE HAS RISES AND FALLS? FOR INSTANCE A LOT OF YOUNGER PEOPLE ARE STARTING TO APPRECIATE DRAG CULTURE MORE AND BE MORE OPEN ABOUT THE BLURRING OF GENDER BOUNDARIES.
I think drag has gone from strength to strength since I’ve started, there has been such a good mix of Artistes coming through. And I think it’s great people are becoming more open to blur gender boundaries.
I think with the younger crowd coming through now RuPauls Drag Race has helped with this, but I always say to people that British drag has got so much history and that people should appreciate and understand that and the queens that perform tirelessly around the country.
