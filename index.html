Drag it Out

This website is the product of a documentary project set in my third year of my University degree (Graphic Design). The images, footage and information collected hopefully give an insight into the current Drag scene in Brighton and the UK in general. These Drag acts are extremely hard working and it was a pleasure to be able to interact with them as well as experience their performances. Click on the title GIFs to explore more about the Queens and performers.
